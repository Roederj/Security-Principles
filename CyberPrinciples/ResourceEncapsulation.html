Resource Encapsulation

To encapsulate resources means to package them in such a way that they only work and operate as originally intended. Resource encapsulation is closely tied to information hiding; so closely, in fact, that the two are often mistaken for one another. The main difference is that while infromation hiding refers to the act of obscuring how something works, resource encapsulation refers to making something work in a particular way.
